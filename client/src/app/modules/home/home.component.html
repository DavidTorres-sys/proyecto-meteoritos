Hoba meteorite

The 60-tonne, 2.7 m-long (8.9 ft) long Hoba meteorite in Namibia is the largest known intact meteorite.

The impact pit made by a 61.9-gram Novato meteorite when it hit the roof of a house on 17 October 2012.

Cape York meteorite

The Cape York meteorite, also known as the Innaanganeq meteorite, is one of the largest known iron meteorites.
